<p><p>AOI Technology encompasses two primary market types: 2D AOI and 3D AOI. </p><p>2D AOI utilizes standard imaging techniques to inspect surfaces and identify defects in PCB assemblies, offering speed and efficiency for mass production lines. In contrast, 3D AOI employs advanced technologies like lasers or structured light to create depth perception, allowing for a more comprehensive inspection of complex components and assuring quality in intricate designs. Both types enhance production quality control, catering to various industry needs.</p></p>
