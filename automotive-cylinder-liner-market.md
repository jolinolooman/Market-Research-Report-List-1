Conversely, aluminum alloys are lighter, enhancing fuel efficiency and reducing overall vehicle weight. They provide good thermal management and corrosion resistance, appealing to modern automotive applications focused on performance and sustainability. Each material serves distinct roles in engine design and efficiency.</p></p>
