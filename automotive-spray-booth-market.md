<p><p>The automotive spray booth market consists of various types designed for efficient paint application. Cross flow paint booths allow air to move horizontally across the workspace, offering simple ventilation. Down draft booths draw air vertically from the ceiling for enhanced paint flow and minimal contaminants. Side down draft booths combine features of both, drawing air from the sides and downwards. Additionally, other market types may include custom or specialty booths tailored for specific automotive applications, enhancing performance and finish quality.</p></p>
