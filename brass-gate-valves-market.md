<p><strong>The Brass Gate Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solder End</li><li>Threaded End</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Brass gate valves, integral to fluid regulation in various applications, are available in two primary market types: solder end and threaded end. Solder end valves feature fittings designed for soldering to pipes, ensuring a seamless connection and minimal leakage, making them ideal for refrigeration and plumbing systems. In contrast, threaded end valves are fitted with screw threads, allowing for easy installation and removal, commonly used in gas and water systems where serviceability is essential. Both types offer durability and reliability in fluid control.</p></p>
