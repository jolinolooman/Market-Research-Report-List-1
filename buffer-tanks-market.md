<p><ul><li>Chilled Water Buffer Tanks (CBT)</li><li>Hot Water Buffer Tanks (HBT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The buffer tanks market encompasses two primary types: Chilled Water Buffer Tanks (CBT) and Hot Water Buffer Tanks (HBT). CBTs store excess chilled water, optimizing cooling systems in commercial and industrial applications, ensuring even temperature distribution and energy efficiency. HBTs, on the other hand, store heated water to meet peak demand, enhancing heating system performance. Both types play crucial roles in HVAC applications, enabling better energy management, operational flexibility, and prolonged equipment lifespan.</p></p>
