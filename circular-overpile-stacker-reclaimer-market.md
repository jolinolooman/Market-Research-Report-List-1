Bucket Wheel stackers utilize rotating buckets to gather and heap materials in a circular formation, offering efficient bulk handling. In contrast, Scraper stackers deploy a continuous belt with scraper blades to collect and reclaim materials. Both types are essential in sectors like mining and construction, streamlining operations by managing large volumes of material with effectiveness and precision in stockpile management.</p></p>
