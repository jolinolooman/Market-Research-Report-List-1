<p><p>The compact drives market can be categorized into low-voltage, medium-voltage, and high-voltage sectors, each serving distinct applications and industries. Medium-voltage drives, ranging from 1 kV to 7.2 kV, cater to larger industrial applications, enhancing efficiency and control.
