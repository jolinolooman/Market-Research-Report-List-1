<p><p>The curved glass market includes several key types. Annealed curved glass, known for its strength and simplicity, is commonly used in architectural applications. Laminated curved glass offers enhanced safety and security by sandwiching a protective layer between two glass sheets, making it ideal for areas requiring durability. Double glazed curved glass consists of two glass panes separated by an insulating space, providing energy efficiency and sound insulation, making it popular in modern building designs. Each type serves distinct functional and aesthetic purposes.</p></p>
