<p><strong>The Digital Temperature Controller-Limiters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Switch Controller</li><li>Proportional Controller</li><li>Pid Controller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital Temperature Controller-Limiters come in various types, primarily including Switch Controllers, Proportional Controllers, and PID Controllers. Switch Controllers operate on a simple on/off mechanism, quickly toggling the system to maintain a set temperature. Proportional Controllers adjust the output based on the difference between the setpoint and actual temperature, providing smoother control. PID Controllers combine proportional, integral, and derivative actions for precise temperature regulation, improving stability and response time for complex heating and cooling systems across various applications.</p></p>
