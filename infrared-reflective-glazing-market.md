<p><strong>The Infrared Reflective Glazing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct-Coat IRR Glazing</li><li>IRR Glazing with IR Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Infrared Reflective Glazing market consists of two primary types: Direct-Coat IRR Glazing and IRR Glazing with IR Film. Direct-Coat IRR Glazing involves the application of a thin layer of infrared-reflective material directly onto the glass surface, enhancing energy efficiency by reflecting infrared radiation. On the other hand, IRR Glazing with IR Film incorporates a specialized film applied to the glass, providing additional insulation and reflecting heat while maintaining visibility. Both types aim to improve indoor comfort and reduce energy costs in commercial and residential applications.</p></p>
