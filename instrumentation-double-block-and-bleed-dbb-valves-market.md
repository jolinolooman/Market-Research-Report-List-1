<p><p>Instrumentation Double Block and Bleed (DBB) valves are critical for ensuring safety and isolating sections of piping. The market includes different types: </p><p>- **Single Block** valves provide basic isolation.</p><p>- **Single Block and Bleed** valves allow for draining or venting between the block and the next isolation point.</p><p>- **Double Block** valves offer two isolation points but lack a bleed.</p><p>- **Double Block and Bleed** valves have both dual isolation and a bleed system, ensuring comprehensive safety in high-pressure applications.</p></p>
