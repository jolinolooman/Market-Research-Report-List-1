<p><strong>The Laminar Flow Bench Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vertical</li><li>Horizontal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laminar flow bench market consists of two primary types: vertical and horizontal. Vertical laminar flow benches feature airflow directed from the top downward, creating a sterile working environment ideal for applications like tissue culture and microbiology. In contrast, horizontal laminar flow benches direct air across the workspace from the back to the front, offering a wide surface area for larger tasks. Both types effectively maintain cleanliness and reduce contamination risks in laboratories and cleanrooms.</p></p>
