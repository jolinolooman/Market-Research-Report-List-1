<p><p>The Low Resistance Backflow Preventer market encompasses two primary types: Double Diaphragm and Single Diaphragm systems. Double Diaphragm devices utilize two separate diaphragms to provide enhanced protection against backflow, ensuring higher reliability and reduced leakage risks. In contrast, Single Diaphragm systems feature a single diaphragm that effectively prevents backflow while offering a more compact design. Both types cater to various applications, such as plumbing, irrigation, and industrial processes, emphasizing the need for efficient fluid control and safety in fluid systems.</p></p>
