<p><p>The manhole saw market can be categorized into two main types based on the shape of the cut: circular cut and square cut. Circular cut saws are designed to create round openings, often used for standard manholes, ensuring a smooth and consistent edge. In contrast, square cut saws are utilized for precise, angular openings, often needed for specific configurations or custom installations. Each type caters to different installation requirements, preferences, and regulatory standards in construction and maintenance projects.</p></p>
