<p><strong>The Molded Pulp Liner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Pressing</li><li>Dry Pressing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The molded pulp liner market is primarily categorized into two types: wet pressing and dry pressing. Wet pressing involves forming pulp molds using water, which creates a dense, strong product suitable for packaging and protective applications. Conversely, dry pressing utilizes less moisture, resulting in lighter, more porous liners, ideal for applications requiring less weight and enhanced breathability. Both methods cater to various industries, including food packaging, electronics, and agriculture, by offering sustainable and biodegradable alternatives to traditional materials.</p></p>
