<p><p>Multi-layer security markets can be categorized into three types: proactive, detective, and reactive. Proactive security focuses on prevention, implementing measures to avert potential threats before they occur. Detective security aims to identify and monitor events or anomalies, allowing organizations to respond to incidents as they arise. Reactive security comes into play after a breach or incident has occurred, emphasizing response and recovery efforts. Together, these layers create a comprehensive security strategy that enhances an organization’s resilience against threats.</p></p>
