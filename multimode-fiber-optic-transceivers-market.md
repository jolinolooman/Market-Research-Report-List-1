<p><p>The multimode fiber optic transceiver market includes various types designed for different data transmission needs. SFP (Small Form-factor Pluggable) transceivers are compact and widely used for short-range communications. SFF (Small Form-factor) offers similar benefits with a slightly different design. XFP (10 Gigabit Small Form-factor Pluggable) supports higher data rates for more demanding applications. CXP transceivers are optimized for 100 Gigabit applications, providing high bandwidth. Other types may include MTP/MPO and proprietary solutions, catering to diverse networking demands.</p></p>
