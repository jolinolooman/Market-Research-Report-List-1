<p><p>The newborn screening software market encompasses various technologies for early detection of metabolic and genetic disorders. Tandem mass spectrometry identifies metabolic disorders by analyzing amino acids and acylcarnitines. Pulse oximetry screens for congenital heart defects by measuring oxygen levels in the blood. Enzyme-based assays assess enzyme activity linked to specific disorders, while DNA assays detect genetic mutations. Electrophoresis separates biological molecules to identify conditions caused by hemoglobin variants and other proteins, ensuring timely intervention and improved health outcomes for newborns.</p></p>
