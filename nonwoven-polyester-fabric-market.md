<p><p>The nonwoven polyester fabric market is classified into single-layer and multilayer types. Single-layer nonwoven fabrics consist of a single, uniform layer, providing basic functionality and cost-effectiveness for applications like disposable products and basic protective gear. In contrast, multilayer nonwoven fabrics feature multiple layers, enhancing properties such as durability, filtration, and moisture resistance, making them suitable for advanced applications in healthcare, automotive, and construction. Each type caters to specific needs, influencing performance, durability, and overall cost.</p></p>
