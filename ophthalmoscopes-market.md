<p><p>The ophthalmoscope market is primarily divided into two types: direct and indirect inspection. Direct inspection ophthalmoscopes provide a straightforward, one-to-one view of the retina, allowing for detailed examination of its surface structures. They are ideal for quick assessments. In contrast, indirect inspection ophthalmoscopes offer a wider field of view and better depth perception, making them suitable for comprehensive evaluations and diagnosis of retinal diseases. Both methods are essential for eye care professionals in diagnosing and treating ocular conditions effectively.</p></p>
