<p><p>The Panel PC market consists of two primary types: fan-enabled and fan-less systems. Fan-enabled Panel PCs use built-in fans for active cooling, making them suitable for environments with high heat generation, ensuring reliable performance under heavy workloads. In contrast, fan-less Panel PCs employ passive cooling, utilizing innovative designs to dissipate heat without moving parts, which enhances durability and reduces noise. These systems are ideal for applications requiring silent operation and lower maintenance, often found in industrial and commercial settings.</p></p>
