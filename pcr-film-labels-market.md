<p><p>The PCR film labels market is segmented into three main types: PET, PE, and PP. PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) labels offer high clarity and durability, making them ideal for premium products. PE (Polyethylene) labels are lightweight, flexible, and resistant to moisture, suitable for applications requiring adaptability. PP (Polypropylene) labels are known for their excellent printability and resistance to chemicals and UV light, commonly used in food packaging. Each type caters to specific industry needs while promoting sustainability through the use of post-consumer recycled materials.</p></p>
