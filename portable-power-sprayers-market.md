<p><strong>The Portable Power Sprayers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Coupling Type</li><li>Belt Driven Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable power sprayers market features two main types: direct coupling and belt-driven sprayers. Direct coupling sprayers have a motor directly attached to the pump, offering higher efficiency and compact design, which makes them suitable for small-scale applications. In contrast, belt-driven sprayers utilize a belt and pulley system to connect the motor to the pump, allowing for increased flexibility and power, making them ideal for larger agricultural or industrial tasks. Both types cater to varying user needs and operational contexts.</p></p>
