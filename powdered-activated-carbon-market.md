<p><ul><li>Physical Method</li><li>Chemical Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The powdered activated carbon (PAC) market can be categorized into two primary types: physical method and chemical method. The physical method involves activation processes like steam or air that enhance the carbon's porous structure, improving adsorption properties. In contrast, the chemical method uses chemical agents to create surface functional groups, further optimizing adsorption capabilities for specific contaminants. Each method caters to different applications, influencing PAC's effectiveness in water treatment, air purification, and industrial processes.</p></p>
