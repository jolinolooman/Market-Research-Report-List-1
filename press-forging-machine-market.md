<p><p>The press forging machine market encompasses various types such as closed die forging, open die forging, and extrusion. Closed die forging involves shaping metal within a sealed mold, ensuring high precision and uniformity. Open die forging utilizes flat dies to create larger, less intricate shapes, allowing more flexibility. Extrusion forces material through a die to produce long shapes with consistent cross-sections. Other forms may include specific applications, such as impact forging and ring rolling, catering to diverse manufacturing needs.</p></p>
