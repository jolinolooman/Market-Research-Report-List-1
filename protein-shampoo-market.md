<p><p>The protein shampoo market features two main types: amino acid and hydrolyzed protein shampoos. Amino acid shampoos utilize essential building blocks of proteins to nourish and strengthen hair, enhancing its health and resilience. Hydrolyzed protein shampoos contain proteins that have been broken down into smaller peptides, allowing for better absorption into the hair shaft. Both types aim to repair damage, improve elasticity, and provide hydration, catering to consumers seeking effective solutions for various hair concerns.</p></p>
