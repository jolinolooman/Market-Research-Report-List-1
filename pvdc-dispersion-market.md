<p><p>The PVDC (Polyvinylidene Chloride) dispersion market is categorized into three main viscosity types: low, medium, and high viscosity. Low viscosity dispersions are ideal for applications requiring easy spreadability and quick drying, often used in coatings and adhesives. Medium viscosity offers a balance, suitable for various packaging and food protection applications. High viscosity dispersions are utilized in more demanding scenarios, providing enhanced barrier properties and durability, often in specialty packaging and industrial coatings, where thicker films are advantageous.</p></p>
