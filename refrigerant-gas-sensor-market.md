<p><strong>The Refrigerant Gas Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electrochemical</li><li>Semiconductor</li><li>Pellistor</li><li>Infrared Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The refrigerant gas sensor market comprises several types, each suited for specific applications. Electrochemical sensors detect refrigerants through chemical reactions, offering high sensitivity. Semiconductor sensors use materials that change conductivity when exposed to gases, making them effective for low concentration detection. Pellistor sensors, based on catalytic combustion, are designed for flammable gases and provide reliable measurements. Infrared sensors detect gases based on their absorption of infrared light, offering non-contact and accurate detection, suitable for various refrigerants used in HVAC systems.</p></p>
