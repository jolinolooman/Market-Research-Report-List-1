<p><p>The sit-to-stand lift market includes two primary types: manual and electric sit-to-stand lifts. Manual sit-to-stand lifts require physical effort from caregivers to assist individuals in transitioning from sitting to standing positions, offering a cost-effective solution for those with limited mobility. In contrast, electric sit-to-stand lifts utilize motorized mechanisms for smoother and easier lifts, enhancing user comfort and reducing caregiver strain. Both types are essential in healthcare settings, facilitating patient mobility and improving overall quality of care.</p></p>
