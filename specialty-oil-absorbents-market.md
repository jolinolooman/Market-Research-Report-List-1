<p><p>The specialty oil absorbents market comprises three main types: natural organic, natural inorganic, and synthetic materials. Natural organic absorbents, derived from materials like peat or cellulose, are biodegradable and environmentally friendly. Natural inorganic absorbents utilize minerals such as clay, offering high absorption capacity and thermal stability. Synthetic materials, made from polymers, provide enhanced absorption efficiency and durability, often tailored for specific applications. Each type serves distinct needs in industrial spill response and environmental cleanup, balancing performance with environmental considerations.</p></p>
