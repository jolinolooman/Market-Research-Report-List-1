<p><ul><li>Extensive Vegetated Roof</li><li>Semi-Intensive Vegetated Roof</li><li>Intensive Vegetated Roof</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vegetated roof market comprises three primary types: Extensive, Semi-Intensive, and Intensive. Extensive vegetated roofs feature lightweight plants and minimal substrate, requiring low maintenance, ideal for energy efficiency. Semi-Intensive roofs combine elements of both extensive and intensive systems, supporting a wider variety of plants and moderate maintenance needs. Intensive vegetated roofs promote lush landscapes with deep substrates, allowing for a diverse range of plants and recreational use, but necessitating higher maintenance and irrigation. Each type offers distinct benefits aligned with sustainability goals.</p></p>
