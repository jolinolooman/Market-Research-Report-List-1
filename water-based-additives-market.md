<p><p>The water-based additives market includes various types that enhance the performance of coatings, paints, and inks. Wetting and dispersing additives improve the spread and stability of pigments, ensuring uniform color and texture. Defoaming additives reduce surface bubbles during mixing and application, preventing defects. Rheology additives control the flow and viscosity, ensuring optimal application properties. Together, these additives play a crucial role in enhancing the efficiency and quality of water-based formulations across multiple industries.</p></p>
